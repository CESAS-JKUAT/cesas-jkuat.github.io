About CESAS Research Group

Welcome to the Centre of Excellence for Sustainable Agrifood Systems (CESAS) CESAS! Our interdisciplinary team focuses on cutting-edge research in the fields of agricultural science. We are dedicated to exploring innovative solutions to problems in the communities, and we collaborate with industry partners and other academic institutions to bring our research to life.

Research Areas

  1. Agricultural production
    • Crop production
    • Animal production
  2. Climate change and environment
    • Generation of climate change scenarios
    • Suitability assessment
    • Determination of adaption mechanisms
